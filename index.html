Safe to Print
Compound identity (CAS, mol weight, amino acids) is factual chemistry. Receptor binding uses biochemical language. Friendly blurb says who developed it and the legal framework — no outcomes. Vial photo and logo add professionalism and brand trust.
Deliberately Omitted
No outcomes ("weight loss," "blood sugar"). No dosing (2.5mg escalation). No brand comparisons ("like Mounjaro"). No pricing. The friendly blurb adds warmth without making claims — it talks about the SCIENCE of the compound, not what it does for patients.
Human Touch Strategy
The warm intro copy ("next-generation," "novel approach") conveys excitement without claims. Elegant icons (molecule, grid, target) make sections scannable and premium. The friendly blurb box with the info icon creates a conversational moment. The vial photo builds trust — this is a real product.
Variants on One Card
Both 30mg and 60mg shown on one card (same compound, different strengths). Saves print costs and avoids doctor confusion. The "Available Strengths" section with pill tags is clean and doesn't suggest dosing — it just shows what's in the catalog.

Human Touch (Back)
The physician trust banner (dark bar with stethoscope icon) creates warmth and speaks to the audience — "designed for practitioners." It's a brand voice statement, not a claim. The elegant SVG icons on every storage card and feature tag make the card feel premium and scannable, not sterile.
QR Bridge
Everything on the card is standalone defensible. The QR promises ACCESS TO INFORMATION ("complete compound profile"), not outcomes. Feature tags with mini icons (recon guide, evidence, safety, order) tell the doctor exactly what they'll get — not what the compound will do.
Icon Strategy
All icons are inline SVG (no external dependencies). Lucide-style 24x24 viewBox, 1.5-2px stroke, round caps. They match the brand: gold (#C5A55A) for section headers, muted (#8A8A8A) for supporting elements. Consistent visual language across front and back creates a cohesive, elegant feel.
Logo Placement
Real SVG monogram appears 3 times: front header (26px), back header (22px), and back footer (14px watermark). The gold accent band across the top of both sides ties the brand together. Wordmark "Sublime Vitality" in Playfair Display next to the monogram on both sides.
